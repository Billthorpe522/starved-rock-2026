The Show

Twenty-three years of bringing collectors, dealers, and the curious together in one room. Free admission. Free artifact evaluation by experts on the floor. Authentic pre-1900 Native American material only.

About Scott

Scott Carruthers hosts the 23rd Annual Starved Rock Native American Artifact Show alongside Jeff Hipskind and Todd Ferguson, carrying on the show founded by Bruce Filbrandt.

Held every summer at Grand Bear Resort in Utica, IL, the show brings 117 tables of authentic pre-1900 Native American material — points, pottery, bannerstones, and pipes — to collectors and dealers from across the Midwest.

— Scott Carruthers, Show Host
